Training & Consultation

Professional development, school consultation, and organizational support

Overview

In addition to direct clinical services, I offer professional training and consultation to mental health practitioners, schools, and organizations. Drawing on over 20 years of clinical experience and my academic role as Department Chair and Associate Professor at Carroll College, I provide evidence-based guidance to help professionals and systems better support the young people and families they serve.

Whether you are a school seeking guidance on student behavioral health, an organization looking to improve mental health support, or a practitioner seeking supervision or training, I am available to collaborate and share expertise.

🎓

Professional Training

Training workshops and presentations for mental health practitioners, educators, and other professionals on evidence-based interventions, child and adolescent psychology, experiential therapy, and best practices in working with youth and families.

🏫

School Consultation

Consultation services for schools and educational settings to support student mental health, behavioral challenges, and neurodevelopmental concerns. I collaborate with teachers, administrators, and school counselors to develop practical, effective strategies.

🏢

Organizational Consultation

Consultation for organizations, agencies, and community programs serving children, adolescents, and families. Services include program development, staff training, and guidance on implementing evidence-based mental health practices.

🔬

Clinical Supervision

Supervision and mentorship for graduate students, practicum trainees, and early-career mental health professionals seeking to develop their clinical skills in child and adolescent psychology, experiential therapy, and assessment.

Areas of Expertise

Training and consultation services draw on my extensive clinical and academic background, including expertise in:

  • Child and adolescent psychology
  • Evidence-based therapeutic interventions (CBT, trauma-focused CBT, behavioral activation)
  • Experiential and adventure-based therapy
  • Neurodevelopmental disorders (ADHD, ASD)
  • Psychological assessment and evaluation
  • Behavioral challenges in educational and community settings
  • Youth in corrections and high-risk populations
  • Family systems and parent support strategies

Academic Background

As Department Chair and Internship Coordinator for the Psychology Department at Carroll College, I bring a rigorous academic foundation to all training and consultation engagements — bridging the latest research with practical, real-world application.

Tailored to Your Needs

Each training or consultation engagement is customized to the unique needs of the individual, school, or organization. Please reach out to discuss how I can best support your goals.

Presentations & Workshops

I am available for presentations and workshops for professional groups, community organizations, and educational institutions. Topics can be tailored to your audience and may include:

  • 🧠
    Understanding Child and Adolescent Mental Health — an overview of common psychological concerns affecting youth and evidence-based approaches to treatment.
  • 🌲
    Experiential and Adventure Therapy — practical applications of activity-based therapeutic techniques for practitioners and educators.
  • 📋
    Neurodevelopmental Disorders in Schools and Clinics — identification, assessment, and evidence-based interventions for ADHD and Autism Spectrum Disorder.
  • 👨‍👩‍👧
    Supporting Families in Crisis — strategies for professionals working with high-risk youth and families across community and correctional settings.
  • 🏫
    School-Based Mental Health Consultation — practical guidance for school professionals on addressing behavioral and emotional concerns in educational environments.
