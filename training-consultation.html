Training & Consultation

Educational services related to child and adolescent health and development

Overview

In addition to direct clinical services, I provide educational and training services to organizations seeking to improve their knowledge of issues related to child and adolescent health and development. Drawing on clinical practice and academic experience, I offer presentations, workshops, and consultation that are practical, evidence-based, and tailored to the audience.

Services are available for schools, mental health practitioners, community organizations, and other groups looking for thoughtful support around youth mental health, development, and intervention.

Available Trainings

Select a topic to view a description.

Understanding Child and Adolescent Mental Health

An overview of common psychological concerns affecting youth, along with practical and evidence-based approaches to understanding and supporting children and adolescents.

Experiential and Adventure Therapy

A closer look at activity-based and experiential interventions, including how these approaches can be used to increase engagement and translate therapeutic concepts into real-world practice.

Neurodevelopmental Disorders in Schools and Clinics

Training focused on the identification, assessment, and support of youth with ADHD, autism spectrum disorder, and other neurodevelopmental concerns across educational and clinical settings.

Supporting Families in Crisis

Strategies for professionals working with high-risk youth and families, with attention to communication, collaboration, and intervention across community, school, and correctional settings.

School-Based Mental Health Consultation

Practical guidance for school professionals on responding to behavioral and emotional concerns, coordinating support, and promoting student well-being in educational environments.

Consultation Services

  • 🏫
    School Consultation for student mental health, behavioral concerns, and practical support planning in educational settings.
  • 🏢
    Organizational Consultation for agencies and community programs serving children, adolescents, and families.
  • 🔬
    Clinical Supervision and Mentorship for graduate trainees and early-career professionals developing clinical skills.

Tailored to Your Audience

Each training or consultation engagement is customized to the needs of the individual group, school, or organization, with an emphasis on usable information and real-world application.

Academic Background

As Department Chair and Internship Coordinator for the Psychology Department at Carroll College, I bring current teaching, supervision, and research perspectives to every training and consultation engagement.
