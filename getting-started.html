Getting Started

What to expect when you begin the process

My Approach

I utilize a compassionate, strengths-based, and evidence-driven approach, emphasizing the development of practical tools that empower clients to "become their own experts." This philosophy is designed to help clients build skills that will eventually allow them to graduate from therapy and continue progressing independently.

Whether you are seeking support for your child, adolescent, or family, the process of getting started is designed to be straightforward and welcoming.

The Intake Process

1

Initial Contact

Call my office at (406) 459-2931 to receive more information or to schedule an intake appointment. After reaching out, I or my administrative staff will return calls typically within 48 business hours.

2

Complete Intake Paperwork

Once an appointment is scheduled, you will receive access to a secure online portal to complete initial paperwork in advance. This helps ensure that your first session is focused on your goals and concerns, rather than administrative tasks.

3

First Session

The first session typically lasts one hour and focuses on understanding the client's background, concerns, strengths, and goals for therapy. This is a collaborative conversation designed to establish rapport and begin building a personalized treatment plan.

4

Ongoing Therapy

Therapy sessions are commonly scheduled weekly to start, though alternate frequencies can be discussed based on each client's needs and insurance. Sessions are tailored to develop evidence-based skills, aiming to foster independence in managing challenges. As clients gain confidence and experience symptom relief, session frequency is reduced over time.

5

Graduation from Services

My goal is to help clients and families acquire the knowledge and resilience they need to navigate life's obstacles, making them their "own experts" and equipping them to graduate from therapy efficiently and confidently.
