Getting Started

What the first contact and intake process typically look like

What to Expect

The process of getting started is designed to be straightforward, collaborative, and informative. After an initial phone connection, I can provide more detailed information about services and specialties, learn about the presenting concerns, and help determine whether my practice is a good fit for your treatment goals.

If it seems like a good fit, the next steps typically include scheduling an intake appointment, completing paperwork through the secure portal, and using the first session to begin building a thoughtful plan for care.

New Client Quick Start

Phone calls are typically returned within 48 business hours. Once an appointment is scheduled, paperwork is completed online through the secure portal so the first session can stay focused on concerns, strengths, and goals.

The Intake Process

1

Initial Contact

Call my office at (406) 459-2931 to receive more information or to schedule an intake appointment. I or my administrative staff typically return phone calls within 48 business hours.

2

Discuss Concerns and Fit

During the initial contact, we can talk about who is seeking services, the main concerns bringing you in, and whether my services and specialties are likely to be a good fit for your goals.

3

Complete Intake Paperwork

Once an appointment is scheduled, you will receive access to a secure online portal to complete initial paperwork in advance. This helps keep the first appointment focused on your goals and concerns rather than administrative tasks.

4

First Session

The first session typically lasts one hour and focuses on understanding background, concerns, strengths, and goals for therapy. It is a collaborative conversation designed to establish rapport and begin building a personalized treatment plan.

5

Ongoing Therapy

Therapy commonly begins on a once-weekly basis, though frequency can be adjusted based on individual needs and insurance. As progress is made, visits are reduced over time with the goal of helping clients become more confident and independent.
